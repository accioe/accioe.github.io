Behind my Art

I am a freshman in college, born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. I have been doing art basically my entire life, while my photography career has been in the works for about four years now. A childhood full of camping and playing outside hatched an appreciation for nature and animals, which influenced my art and will for years to come.

My work is mostly photography and digital artwork. I tend to stay in the realm of animal and landscape photography. Overall, my work is about appreciating the smaller, more overlooked, beauty of the world. I often hope that, after someone looks at and experiences my work, that they try to see the beauty in their lives that they previously failed to notice.
